Technology assisted reinsurance placement

ndevi is a B2B startup that provides technology assisted bargaining solutions for reinsurance placement. We are creating systems that can reliably replicate the outcomes of conventional methods in a fraction of the time and human resources. Our system can help firms manage the impact of information asymmetries and also generate market level micro data from various product segments.

We are not trying to create a marketplace to match reinsurance capacities with needs. We are creating a new conversation channel in the existing marketplace. We provide a choice of simple and automated bid comparison rules to help negotiating parties reach agreement quickly. {{/*Conventional reinsurance placement is time and resource intensive. Our view is that, apart from a few rare and highly complex scenarios, most reinsurance deals have become quite commoditized, and should take no more than a few days to place. */}}

{{/*We have base solutions targeted at three levels of reinsurance placement:
  • Primary placement: Initial selection of a lead reinsurer (treaty).
  • One shot secondary placement of the residual reinsurance need after leader selection for treaty agreements.
  • Individual facultative placements.
Solutions are developed thinking of insurers or reinsurance brokers as the party starting a placement process. The secondary placement solution can also be used by reinsurers themselves in the retrocession market. *}}
